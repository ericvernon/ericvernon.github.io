Research

I am a doctoral student at Osaka Metropolitan University (previously known as Osaka Prefecture University) within the Graduate School of Informatics. I completed my Master's Degree in Spring 2022, and plan to defend my doctoral thesis in Spring 2027.

My main research interest is explainable artificial intelligence. Currently, I am active in a small subfield called "partial interpretability", which tries to combine inherently interpretable methods (glass box models, e.g.) with black box methods in order to balance performance with explainability.
